Terms of Use

ShiftPay Pro pay calculations are estimates only. Always verify against your official payslip.

Why doesn't my calculated pay exactly match my payslip?

ShiftPay Pro uses the tax rates and deduction rules you've entered in Pay & Tax Settings. Small differences can occur due to rounding, employer-specific agreements, or mid-year tax adjustments. Check that your tax code, KiwiSaver rate and pay cycle are set correctly. The app provides an estimate — your official payslip from your employer is always the authoritative figure.

How do penal rates work in ShiftPay Pro?

ShiftPay Pro splits each shift at the relevant thresholds — 8pm for night rate and midnight for weekend rate — and applies the correct multiplier to each portion. Bonus hours are added at your base rate, matching the NZNO MECA payslip structure. All thresholds and multipliers are editable in Pay & Tax Settings to match your employment agreement.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → find ShiftPay Pro and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. Lifetime purchases are permanent and never require cancellation.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your shift and pay data is stored locally on your device. ShiftPay Pro does not upload your pay or hours data to any server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
