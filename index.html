About

Made with
care,
for the people
who show up.

DJ&A Digital Studio is an independent app studio focused on the needs of shift workers. We build tools that are offline-first, privacy-respecting, and designed to work in the real conditions of shift work — no signal, no time, no patience for complexity.

Every app we make stores your data on your device. We don't build accounts, we don't run databases of your pay or hours, and we don't sell your data. What you enter stays with you.

We support workers across New Zealand, Australia, the UK, the US, Canada, the Philippines, Japan, Singapore, and more.
