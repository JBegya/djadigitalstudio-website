3. This Website

djadigitalstudio.com does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or third-party trackers. We do not build a profile of your visits to this site, and no advertising or tracking pixels are loaded.

The only data we receive from the website is what you choose to send us — for example, by emailing an address listed on our Contact page. We use that information solely to respond to your enquiry.

4. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information you've shared with us, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

  • New Zealand — under the Privacy Act 2020, you can request access to or correction of personal information we hold about you by contacting us below, or complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner (privacy.org.nz).
  • European Economic Area / UK — under the GDPR / UK GDPR, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, or to request deletion of data associated with a specific app, see our Data Deletion Request page or email us directly.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps and website are intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. International Data

DJ&A Digital Studio Limited is based in New Zealand. Where we process personal information (for example, an email you send us), it may be handled using service providers located outside your country. We take reasonable steps to ensure any such processing meets applicable data protection standards.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
